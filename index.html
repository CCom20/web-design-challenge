Welcome to Weather Analysis 1.0

Weather Analysis

The Weather Analysis project looks at relationships between latitude and four other variables: cloudiness, wind speed, humidity, and max temperature. The question being answerd is: "Does the weather change as you get closer to the equator?"

Bottom-Line: There's strong correlation between latitude and max temperature. Generally, as you move closer to the equator, temperatures rise.

About the Project: Measurements for 500 cities were taken on January 5, 2017, and the data was pulled from OpenWeather API. After pulling the data, we used Pandas and Matplotlib to clean and graph the data. You can view the final csv on the data page.
