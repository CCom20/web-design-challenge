Latitude and Humidity

As expected, there is strong correlation between latitude and temperature. However, there are outliers which are likely due to weather patterns. Another note is the warmer temperatures in the southern hemisphere. Perhaps it's worth seeing if this is related to the Earth's axis tilt. To do this, we could take measurements either daily or weekly and see if temperatures shift between the Northern and Southern hemispheres.

Bottom-Line: There's strong correlation between latitude and temperature.
